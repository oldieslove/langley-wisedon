Benefits

There's so many great benefits at Langley, there's not enough room on this page to list them all. So let's just say this – if you want great people, you've got to have great benefits.

Health

  • Two medical plan options
  • Dental coverage
  • Vision coverage
  • Fitness membership reimbursement
  • Free flu shots

Leave

  • 12 paid holidays (including your birthday and the day after Thanksgiving)
  • Paid vacation (three weeks for full time employees, and one and a half weeks for part time employees)
  • Paid sick time for all employees

Finance
